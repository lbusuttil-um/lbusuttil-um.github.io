Georgiana is a Assistant Professor in the Computer Science department at Colgate University and a CS Education Researcher. Her research interests include ways to support students' understanding of programming such as automated formative feedback and notional machines, and introducing non-functional aspects of programming  in introductory courses, such as code style with a particular focus on procedural decomposition.
